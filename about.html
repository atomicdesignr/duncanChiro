Dr. Susan Duncan
Certified Chiropractic Sports Physician, Liscened Acupuncturer

Susan Duncan D.C., C.C.S.P., F.I.A.C.A. received her Bachelors of Science degree from Pennsylvania State University. She continued her education at Palmer College of Chiropractic to graduate Cum Laude. After graduation, Dr. Duncan worked as a falculty member for 10 years while starting her own practice here in the Quad Cities. During Dr. Duncan's high school career, she participated in many sports. To this day, she is still an avid sport enthusiast.

Dr. Taylor Overmohle, Certified Chiropractic, DOT Physician

Taylor Overmohle, D.C. graduated from Palmer college of Chiropractic with her bachelors of Science as well as her doctorate degree. She has advanced training in the McKenzie Technique and Flexion Distraction as well as soft tissue techniques Graston and Nimmo. She is also a medical examiner for DOT physicals.
