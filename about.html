Dr. Susan Duncan
Certified Chiropractic Sports Physician, Actupuncturist

Susan Duncan D.C., C.C.S.P., F.I.A.C.A. graduated from Penn. State University. She continued her education at Palmer College of Chiropractic to graduate Cum Laude. Dr. Taylor Overmohle, Certified Chiropractic, DOT Physician

Taylor Overmohle, D.C. graduated from Palmer college of Chiropractic with her bachelors of Science as well as her doctorate degree.
